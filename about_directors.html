Founder & Principal

Mrs. Shashi Gupta
(Founder & Principal)


Mrs. Shashi Gupta has been a teacher and administrator for over two decades. Starting as a kinder garten teacher at Nasr School in 1984, she grew to become the Head of Nasr School for Boys in 1998. During her teaching experience, she realized that children with learning difficulties were side lined by main-stream education. She left Nasr in 2001 to start VIDHYANJALI, a place where children would willingly go to study and learn. Dedicated to making VIDHYANJALI A SUCCESSFUL MISSION, Mrs. Gupta and her team of trained teachers help children achieve their potential as they learn at their own pace.

She answers questions from readers on their psychological and personal problems.
